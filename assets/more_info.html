AI email helper

Info page

The idea is that you hook up email functions to an AI and then you can ask it to do things for you. It can do stuff like summarize emails, trash emails, label them (e.g. mark them as read, unread, spam, promotions, etc).


I feel like from here the AI could probably get even more powerful if access to more behaviors (e.g. send messages, act autonomously without explicitly needing to be prompted to do so.) or if I gave it the ability to work over more than one email at a time, or if I used a stronger LLM, but I suppose that's just all aspirations at the moment.


Anyways this would better be served as like a prototype or something, not to be used seriously. I won't guarantee that it won't just trash an email randomly (but only ever one at a time).


Large Language Model Used: llama-4-scout-17b-16e-instruct
